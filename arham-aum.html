The essence of urban living

Make a mark for yourself as a true connoisseur of worldly charms and immaculate style with Arham Aum .... an exclusive combination of vintage luxury and exuberant lifestyle. Nestled among the serene suburb of Matunga, it retains the genteel sense of well being along with the premises for an out of the world living experience. With beautifully designed independent floors, the magnanimous setting is a concise mingling of privacy and modern luxury in the form of opulent spaces and the latest modern amenities. As a high-end tower it features elegantly planned 3 BHK individual apartments on each floor that round up the elusive feeling of exclusivity and set a cozy retreat from the mundane. An architectural splendour, it takes inspiration from the aspirational view of the sea link in the distance and the elaborate horizon, to celebrate style and ambition in harmony. This ensemble of a privileged address, an enriched standard of living and the pristine view of boundless bounty .... are designed to make your life here not just beautiful but pure blissful.. ...
